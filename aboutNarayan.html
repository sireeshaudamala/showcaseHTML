R. K. Narayan

Writer

Rasipuram Krishnaswami lyer Narayanaswami, was an indian writer known for his works set in the fictional South Indian town of Malgudi. He was a leading author of early indian literature in English along with Mulk Raj Anand and Raja Rao.
